The CSI (Container Storage Interface) volume type in Kubernetes is used to integrate external storage systems dynamically with Kubernetes workloads. CSI allows Kubernetes to use third-party storage drivers

Some popular CSI drivers include:

AWS EBS CSI Driver (ebs.csi.aws.com)
Azure Disk CSI Driver (disk.csi.azure.com)
Google Cloud Persistent Disk CSI Driver (pd.csi.storage.gke.io)
Ceph CSI Driver (cephfs.csi.ceph.com)
OpenEBS CSI Driver (openebs-csi)
